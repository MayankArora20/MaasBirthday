Happy Birthday

Happy birthday, big sister!
You don’t look a year older.
You are just a little closer
To looking like our mother!

We share our life,
We share a special bond.
Together since day one,
Together we belong.

You know everything about me
And I know everything about you.
Despite knowing all your secrets
I still completely love you!

We had giggles and laughter.
We had screams and tears.
We were always best friends
And we’ll continue to be
for 100 years more.
